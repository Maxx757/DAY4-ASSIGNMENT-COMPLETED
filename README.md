9. Compare and contrast Agile and Waterfall methodologies. What are the main advantages and disadvantages of each?
10. Agile Methodology
Approach: Iterative and incremental.
Flexibility: Highly adaptable; allows changes even late in the project.
Planning: Focuses on short-term planning through sprints, with adjustments after each iteration.
Collaboration: Strong emphasis on teamwork and continuous communication with stakeholders.
Delivery: Delivers functional increments of the project throughout the process.

Advantages:
1.Flexibility: Easily accommodates changing requirements.
2.Customer-Centric: Frequent feedback ensures the end product meets user needs.
3.Risk Mitigation: Early delivery of incremental parts helps identify and resolve issues sooner.
4.Team Collaboration: Encourages active participation and communication.

Disadvantages:
1.Unpredictable Costs: Continuous changes can make budgeting challenging.
2.Time Pressure: Frequent iterations and deadlines can strain teams.
3.Requires Discipline: Needs experienced and self-organized teams to succeed.

Waterfall Methodology
Approach: Linear and sequential.
Flexibility: Limited; follows a fixed plan and scope once defined.
Planning: Focuses on detailed upfront planning for the entire project.
Collaboration: Interaction with stakeholders is typically concentrated in the beginning and end.
Delivery: Final product is delivered at the end of the process.

Advantages:
1.Predictability: Clear structure with defined milestones and timelines.
2.Simplicity: Easy to understand and manage, especially for smaller projects.
3.Cost Control: Fixed scope helps manage budget more effectively.
4.Documentation: Detailed documentation ensures clarity and compliance.

Disadvantages:
1.Inflexibility: Difficult to accommodate changes once the project starts.
2.Risk of Failure: If an issue arises late in the process, it may require significant rework.
3.Late Feedback: Stakeholders don’t see the product until near completion, increasing the risk of misalignment.
4.Not Suitable for Complex Projects: Linear structure can hinder handling of evolving requirements.
